Smart Wearables: Carry Technology In Your Hands

With the growth of technology, the ‘smart’ world is expanding its reach farther and farther every day. What we considered impossible a couple decades ago is now a reality. And this doesn’t apply to just mobile phones, but also several other gadgets like smartwatches, trackers, and smart headphones. These are all grouped under “Smart Wearables” category. 

Today, everyone wants to equip themselves with a smart device. Here are some great gadgets—apart from phones—if you’re looking to shop for smart wearables.

SmartWatches

Imagine a watch that can think; this is exactly what smart watches are meant to do. They can help you with your daily needs aside from showing you the time. A couple of these cool smartwatches are: 

Apple Watch Series 2

This is one of the best smartwatches you can buy these days. With several features like GPS, 1GB RAM, and amazing apps, this smart wearable is a great pick for tech and fitness enthusiasts. And the best part is that it is waterproof.

Samsung Gear S3

Running on Tizen OS, it is compatible with both Android and iOS. and has a 1.3” Super AMOLED display. It has WiFi, Bluetooth, and 4G connectivity, making it a cool pick for anyone.

Smart Glasses

One of the best ways to experience hardcore gaming is Virtual Reality. This is made possible by a camera that shoots videos in a 360°, making you feel like you’re in an entirely different world. One of the best Virtual Reality glasses is the Samsung Gear VR.

Some other brands that make smart glasses include ANT, Google, Wonder World, Samsung,Mtt  and many more. 

Smart Bands

Smart bands enjoy high demand in the market today, because of their ability to help you out with your fitness. Aside from that, they also come with several other features, depending on the type of model.

Latest Smart Band Brands :Fastrack,Mi,Fitbit,Lenovo,Intex,Garmin 

Moov Now

If you’re only a beginner in your weight loss program, and don’t know where exactly to start, then this little device can help. It is like your own little fitness trainer, and comes at quite an affordable price. 

Unlike other traditional smart bands, this device doesn’t measure stats like steps walked or calories burned. Instead, it tracks your sleep and gives you feedback that helps you grow healthier.

Fitbit Charge 2

This is a great smart wearable device that can track your sleep, steps (along with floors climbed), distance covered, calories burned, and also auto-sleep detection. This smart band has a small OLED display which generally displays the time, but also displays the caller ID. 

Other Brands that make such devices include Lenovo, Fastrack, and fbandz.

Smart Trackers

There are few things worse than losing your phone, car keys, or your purse. And everyone has been through this situation at least once in their lives. But there is a solution.

A smart tracker can save you from such nightmares. You can download an app that connects the tracker to your phone. After that, you will never have to worry about losing anything. Some great brands that design smart trackers include :

Technomart,senseLocate,Portronics,Shopcraze,Tango,Garmin,Wayona,jenix

Smart Headphones

In a generation where almost everything is wireless, sticking close to your phone or PC with wired headphones would become annoying. But there are ways you can get rid of those wires too. 

With smart headphones, you can connect to your laptop or phone through bluetooth. This allows you to work out without wires hanging from your ears. You can also sit at a distance from your PC and enjoy movies with great sound quality. The key feature is that these headphones feature controls mounted right on them; you won’t have to reach your parent device to control volume and playback.Some of the best smart headphones include the Samsung Gear Icon X, Motorola Verve Rider Plus, Techno Frost BS19CG03.With so many smart wearables available in the market today, it is easy to make your life simple. And to top it all off, buying smart wearables is also quite easy. You can place an order for any of the smart wearables online, and have them delivered to you right in the comfort of your home.

Branded Smart Headphones:Motorola,Jabra,Flipfit,Movo,Sacro,Pico
